Robotic Manipulator

Repository
Overview

An aluminum rebuild of the SO-100 robotic arm. The original design uses 3D-printed structural parts; this version replaces them with 20x20 aluminum extrusions.

Hardware
  • Motors: 6x ST3215 serial bus servos
  • Frame: 20x20 aluminum extrusions
  • Printed Parts: PLA/PETG (STLs in repo)
Inference
  • SmolVLA
Picking pens.
Software

Runs on the LeRobot stack.

Teleoperation

Typically arm setups require a second leader arm for teleoperation. I wrote a custom teleoperation method that uses an Xbox controller instead.
